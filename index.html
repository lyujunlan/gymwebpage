Introduction

Doing burpees, pull-ups, push-ups and sprinting while dragging weights – in quick succession –may not be everyone’s cup of tea. But these are regular workouts for members of the Iron Fitness gym. Iron Fitness is a strength and conditioning gym based on a conjugate training system.

Conjugate System

Conjugate means to ‘complete or link’ and is based on increasing muscle tension through three methods. They are:
Maximum effort method, Dynamic effort method and
Repetition effort method.

Good camaraderie

When you stepp into the Iron Fitness gym, you will take aback by the talk and camaraderie amongst the regulars. Unlike some other gyms where people simply go about their own exercise routines without much interaction, the members at Iron Fitness chitchat and joke with each other.
